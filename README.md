# Hospital-Emergency-Room
This project presents a comprehensive data visualization dashboard for a hospital's emergency room activity during January 2024. It provides key insights into patient flow, operational efficiency, and departmental referrals to support data-driven decision-making in healthcare management.
Project Highlights

--Project Highlights
Total Patients Analyzed: 513
Average Wait Time: 363 minutes
Patient Satisfaction Score: 4.96 / 5
Patients Referred to Departments: 214

--Dashboard Features
Admission Status: Breakdown of admitted vs discharged patients
Age Group Distribution: Patient demographics across five age brackets
Department Referrals: Referrals to 9 departments including Cardiology, Orthopedics, and Radiology
Wait Time Analysis: Percentage of patients seen within 30 and 60 minutes
Common Patient Issues: Top medical complaints like fever, chest pain, and fractures
Hourly & Daily Traffic: Patient volume by hour and calendar day view


--Purpose
This dashboard helps hospital administrators and ER managers monitor performance, identify bottlenecks, and improve patient care delivery through real-time insights and historical trends.
